What is Orb Forge?

Orb Forge forges a signature animated orb — a living avatar for your AI agent, bot, app, or profile. One burning comet-ring, shaped by you, exported as a looping animation.

Forge

  • Drag any slider — or click its value and type. Every change renders live.
  • Randomize rolls a new orb from curated ranges; Undo walks it back.
  • Save looks you like as presets; they stay in this browser.
  • Scrub the timeline above the preview, or go fullscreen to inspect it.

Export

  • WebP (recommended): small, smooth, true soft transparency.
  • GIF: plays everywhere, bigger files, hard-edged transparency.
  • Set a target size and the exporter auto-fits resolution, fps, and quality.
  • JSON export captures the exact recipe — commit it, share it, re-import it.

Private by construction

Everything renders and encodes in your browser. No login, no upload, no tracking — the page can’t even phone home; its security policy forbids it.
